Do I feed the turtle or take a shower today? This is calculated based on whether today's julian day is even or odd.
  • Determine the local date
  • Set that date's time to 12:00 UTC
  • Get the julian date. This is the time passed in days since day 0, noon Monday, 4713 B.C. January 1, 12:00:00.0 UTC.
  • Get rid of the fractional part. If the number is even, it's turtle day. If odd, shower day
