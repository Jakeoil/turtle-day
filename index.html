Do I feed the turtle or take a shower today? This is calculated based on whether today's Julian date is even or odd.

  • Determine your system's current date:
  • Set that date's time to 12:00 UTC
  • Ask the U. S. Naval Observatory to give you the Julian Day for that time. This is how many days since Noon UT on Monday, January 1 4713 B.C.
  • Today's Julian Date turns at noon UT today
  • If the number is even, it's turtle day. If odd, shower day
